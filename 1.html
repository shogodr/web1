All the Lights We Cannot See

A novel to live in, learn from, and feel bereft over when the last page is turned, Doerr's magnificiently drawn story seems at once spacious and tightly composed....

Doerr masterfully and knowledgeably re-creates the deprived civilian conditions of war-torn France and the strictly controlled lives of the military occupies.
